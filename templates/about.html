The Project

The World Crisis Database was started as a school assignment at the University of Texas for the class CS 373: Software Engineering. The final project of the class, it appropriately utilizes a wide variety technologies (ranging from Python and Google App Engine, to XML and HTML), and stresses the importance of good teamwork (and good version control software).

In actuality, this project is assigned every semester the class is offered, so there are a bunch of these databases floating around in Google's appspot domain.

However, in our incredibly biased opinion, ours is most certainly the best :P
