ORGANIZATION: World Health Organization

Name

World Health Organization

Kind

Public Health Worldwide

Location

Headquarters at Geneva, Switzerland, but has offices on all 7 continents in 145 different countries.

History

It was established on 7 April 1948, with headquarters in Geneva, Switzerland. Since then, it has played a major role in the eradication of smallpox, yaws, onchocerciasis, SARS and many other infectious diseases.
